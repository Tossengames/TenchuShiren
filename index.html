THE SHINOBI TRIAL

Tenchu: Shiren is a free, fan-made game inspired by the Tenchu series.

You will be tested by questions drawn from the world of Tenchu, its characters, tools, rules, and the mindset of a true shinobi.

  • Some questions challenge your memory
  • Others test your judgment in situations faced by Azuma ninja
  • Familiar characters will observe your choices
  • Earn points to increase your shinobi rank

DISCLAIMER
