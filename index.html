About Tenchu Shiren

Welcome, warrior of the shadows.

Tenchu Shiren is a fan-made project inspired by Tenchu. All rights belong to the original creators. This game is free to play.

Face five situations. Each choice reflects your instinct, honor, and skill as an Azuma ninja.
Rikimaru, Ayame, Rin, and Tatsumaru will observe your decisions. Praise or ridicule awaits.
